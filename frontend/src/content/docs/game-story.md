# Game Story: Shardfall Chronicles

When the **Sky Prism** shattered, seven shards fell across the realm and corrupted each region.
You are the last Warden who can reforge the Prism.

## Boss progression

1. **Varkun, The Hollow Fang**  
   Region: Ember Caverns  
   Theme: Rage and fire

2. **Mirelith, Queen of Thorns**  
   Region: Weeping Wilds  
   Theme: Decay and poison

3. **Dread Admiral Kael**  
   Region: Stormwreck Coast  
   Theme: Lightning and chaos

4. **Nythra, The Veil Seer**  
   Region: Mirror Spire  
   Theme: Illusion and mind control

5. **Azhur Prime, The World Eater**  
   Region: Obsidian Crown  
   Theme: Void and final collapse

## Gameplay loop

- Explore region
- Accept quests
- Fight enemies for experience and items
- Craft and upgrade
- Defeat regional boss
- Recover shard fragment

After all shards are recovered, unlock the final encounter and restore the world.
